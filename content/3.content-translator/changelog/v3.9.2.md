**🚀 Features**

- **Custom AI system prompt**: Customize the [system prompt used for AI translations](/docs/content-translator/providers/ai-translation#custom-system-prompt) via the `ai.systemPrompt` global config option or the `systemPrompt` blueprint property. Replace the built-in translation instructions with your own prompt to fine-tune AI translation behavior per site or per template.
