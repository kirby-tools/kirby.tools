**🐞 Bug Fixes**

- **Translation Coverage on large sites**: Opening the coverage view no longer hits `max_execution_time` on sites with thousands of pages. Field keys are now resolved once per blueprint instead of once per page.
- **Translation Coverage with Staticache**: The coverage view now displays reliably when Staticache is active. Coverage results moved to the plugin's own cache, so they're no longer evicted on every page render or content change.
