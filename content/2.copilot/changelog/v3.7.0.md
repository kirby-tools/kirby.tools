**🚀 Features**

- **Skills**: Define reusable prompt instructions – tone, style, or house rules – in your Kirby config. Editors invoke them from the prompt editor with `@skill://id` mentions via the new typeahead dropdown; their instructions are injected into the system prompt at submit time. See the [skills documentation](/docs/copilot/prompt-dialog/skills) and the [`skills` config reference](/docs/copilot/configuration/global#skills).
