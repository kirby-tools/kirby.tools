Kirby Copilot v2 is a **major release** with full support for Kirby 5. This release brings improvements across the board and requires a new license key. If you already have a license, you receive either a free upgrade to v2 or a 50% discount on your new license. Head over to the [Kirby Tools Hub](https://hub.kirby.tools) to get your discount or read more in the [license compatibility guide](https://kirby.tools/license-compatibility).
